Our Love Story...

A wedding is one among numerous events in everyone’s life. As a couple we have had an atypical order to these events, however, we always knew each event
would be reached. We have become quite the team these past 6 years. Starting from high school lovebirds and now becoming husband and wife. We cannot
wait for you to join us in celebration on our wedding day.

I do!

Brooke Hebert06.02.1993

The first time I met Brooke, I was hanging out at my locker. We were both in high school. At first she paid no attention to me. It seemed as though she wanted nothing to do with me! After a long few months, my funny sense of humor grew on her.

I knew it was love the second I laid my eyes on her. I have fallen in love with a beautifully attractive girl. The thing I love most about Brooke is her competitive personality! Trust me, you’ll never win against her, when playing board games.

I, for one am extremely happy to say I am marrying my high school sweetheart, the girl of my dreams, the mother of my children and best of all my best friend.

Me too!

Nick Germain05.28.1992

The first time I met Nick was through a mutual friend. My first thought was “this guy, with the curly haired fro, is actually quite funny”.

After a few months in the “friend zone” I invited him to a New Years Eve gathering. This is when our Love began to grow!

What I love most about Nick is his love for his family, especially for his kids. I also love his sense of humor and his sensitive side.

I am incredibly lucky to have found my soul mate at such a young age and I am even luckier that I get to spend everyday for the rest of my life with my best friend!

Today and always, beyond tomorrow, I need you beside me, always as my best friend, lover and forever soul mate.
2009

Sept 09: They Met

Brooke and Nick went to the same high school. Although they didn’t officially meet until Brooke was in grade 10 and Nick was in grade 11. The meeting of these two lovebirds was through a mutual friend in 2009 and for that they will always be thankful. When they first met, Nick was “friend zoned” and it took a few months before their relationship really began to bloom.

2010

Jan 16: First Date

Their first date was one that Brooke will never forget! It was a Saturday and the plans were for Nick to go watch Brooke play hockey, the two of them go out for dinner, and then drive her home to meet “the parents”.

Now before I continue let me just mention that Brooke is the oldest daughter and “the favorite” of her siblings.

Her father was so nervous about his “favorite” daughter bringing home the first boyfriend he had started “pre-drinking”….. at 11 am.

May 14: Nick's Prom

On this rainy day, the couple found themselves in the hall of the civic complex. Friends, loud music and flashy lights surrounded them. The two dances all night and sang to all their favorite songs! They had an amazing time. They ended the night watching movies and talking till the sun came up the next day!

2011

May 14: Brooke's Prom

This prom experience was a bit more put together for the couple. By this time they had been together for nearly a year and a half and it was less awkward and more fun! From the bigger dress to the more fancy hair, Brooke put more effort into the way she looked and made sure it was a surprise to Nick. Again friends, loud music and flashy lights surrounded the couple. They ended the party this time by joining the graduating class in a muddy field for a bonfire at their prom party. It was a great night and one that they will always look back on!

2013

Jan 16: McKinley's Here

After 3 amazing years together Brooke and Nick got the surprise that changed their lives in the best way possible. They had a beautiful baby girl on a snowy January night. It was the best anniversary gift either of them could ask for!

2014

May 31: 1st Home

After 5 years together and a beautiful baby girl the couple finally decided it was time to buy their first home! After a long wait, lots of saving, and months of gathering house supplies, the big day was finally here. The couple now had the keys to their first home. They finally had their own personal space and a place where they could “hang their hats”. They were now home owners together!

Dec 16: He Asked?

How did Nick pop the question, one might ask? Well, on this day Nick asked Brooke to open an early Christmas gift. Brooke, hesitant, had a hunch something bigger was going on. McKinley holding this rectangular box, wrapped with a shiny Christmas wrapping paper, insisting she help. As Brooke opens the box, she was expecting to find a smaller box inside or something that would indicate a ring; instead she finds a dozen fresh red roses and one colorful rose. Brooke read the note attached to the flowers “meet me in the kitchen”. By this time Nick had disappeared, so Brooke heads to the kitchen where she finds Nick, down on one knee. He asks, “Will you marry me?” Brooke was speechless, as he slid the ring on her finger she states, “are you serious? Finally!” Now after nearly 5 years, a toddler, a home, and a baby on the way this couple was finally ready to start planning their big day!

2015

May 10: Elliette's Here

After waiting patiently for this bundle of joy to arrive the decision was finally made to set the eviction notice. On Brookes 3rd mothers day the couple welcomed their second baby girl! This family of three has now become a family of four and mother’s day is now something that will forever be a special day!

2016

July 16: To Wed

After 6 and half years together this couple will finally “tie the knot” and they could not be happier! A big thanks goes out to all who supported them and helped make their day possible. It will be at their greatest pleasure to have all their family and friends there and make a lasting impression on their once in a lifetime event.

And so the Adventure
continues
